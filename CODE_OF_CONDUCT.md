# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This repository is intended to support serious technical work in an atmosphere
of professional respect. Participation in issues, pull requests, discussions,
and review should therefore remain constructive, evidence-based, and civil.

## Expected conduct

Participants are expected to:

- engage respectfully and in good faith
- critique ideas, code, and evidence rather than people
- keep technical disagreement precise and proportionate
- avoid harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and abusive language
- respect reasonable boundaries around maintainer time and project scope

## Unacceptable conduct

The following are not acceptable:

- personal attacks or derogatory language
- harassment or discriminatory behavior
- repeated bad-faith argumentation
- deliberate disruption of review or discussion
- publication of private information without permission

## Enforcement

The maintainer may moderate, limit, or close interactions that undermine a
constructive working environment. Repeated or severe violations may result in
removal from repository participation channels.

## Reporting

If a conduct issue arises, report it privately through GitHub rather than
escalating the matter in public discussion.
